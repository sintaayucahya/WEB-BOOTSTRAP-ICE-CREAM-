Matcha Ice Cream

40min Vegetarian, Gluten free 8 servings
Matcha Ice Cream is a delightful frozen treat that combines the rich, creamy texture of heavy cream with the earthy flavor of high-quality matcha powder. This easy-to-make dessert is perfect for warm days or cozy evenings, providing a refreshing escape into the serene world of Japanese tradition. Each scoop offers a vibrant green hue and a unique taste that will impress your guests and satisfy your sweet cravings.

Ingredients

Produce
  • 2 tbsp Matcha powder, high-quality
Baking & Spices
  • 3/4 cup Granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
Dairy
  • 2 cups Heavy cream
  • 1 cup Whole milk
